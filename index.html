Allu Arjun was born on 8 April 1982[a] in a Telugu family in Madras (present-day Chennai) to film producer Allu Aravind and Nirmala. His paternal grandfather was the noted film comedian Allu Ramalingaiah who appeared in over 1000 films.[14][15] Their native place is Palakollu of West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh.[16] Allu Arjun grew up in Chennai before their family moved to Hyderabad in his 20s.[17][18] He is the second of three children. His elder brother Venkatesh is a businessman while his younger brother Sirish is an actor. His paternal aunt is married to Chiranjeevi.[19] His cousins, namely, Ram Charan, Varun Tej, Sai Dharam Tej, and Niharika Konidela are also actors.
